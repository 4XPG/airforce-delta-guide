## General Mission Guides
- Missile count matters a lot especially on Hard difficulty. Enemy fighters could withstand at least three missiles on Hard as early as Mission 6, and most ground targets other than tanks, AA or Missile Pods require at least two missiles to destroy.
- Missile only reloads when the previously launched missile is destroyed whether it dissapates or hits the target/ground. This can be exploited by firing missiles as close as possible from the target to achieve much faster rate of fire than normally allowed.
- Buildings only can be destroyed with missiles. As such, prioritise destroying target buildings before attacking other primary targets whenever possible.
- On normal difficulty or higher enemy ships may have additional missile launchers
- Gun kills on aerial enemies (including large aircraft such as bombers or transport aircraft) gives double money reward per kill. The only requirement that counts as a gun kill is dealing the finishing blow using gun, thus using missiles to damage the target then finishing them with gun is allowed.
